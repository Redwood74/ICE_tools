## Section 5 – Prohibited Uses

The following uses are **categorically prohibited**, regardless of whether
compensation is involved:

(a) **Immigration enforcement operations:** using the Software to assist
    ICE, CBP, DHS, or any federal, state, or local law enforcement or
    immigration enforcement agency in locating, tracking, arresting,
    detaining, deporting, or removing any individual;

(b) **Detention assistance:** using the Software to support the operation,
    management, or administration of immigration detention facilities;

(c) **Surveillance:** using the Software to monitor, track, or compile
    information about individuals for enforcement, intelligence, or
    investigative purposes;

(d) **Operational immigration enforcement:** any use that materially assists
    in the enforcement of immigration law against specific individuals;

(e) **Harmful government use:** use by any government agency for any
    operational enforcement purpose, even if that agency also engages in
    other activities;

(f) **Commercial media operations:** systematic or recurring use by
    commercial news, media, or journalism organizations for revenue-generating
    purposes without a separate paid license (see Section 9);

(g) **Any use in a manner that reverses the protective purpose of this
    Software** – i.e., any use that harms, endangers, or disadvantages
    detained or potentially detained individuals or their families.

The narrow government carve-outs in Section 3(e) and 3(f) apply only to
clearly non-operational, non-enforcement purposes.
